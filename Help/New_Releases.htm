New Releases

When a new version of MH PE iPad is released, you will need to remove the previous version from your iPad, as well as from the iPad Configuration Utility program on your computer.  

 

On the iPad, press & hold the MH PE icon until it wiggles, and then press the “x” to delete the app from the iPad.  

On the computer, launch the iPad Configuration Utility program and go to Applications.  Select the MH SAM PE app and press the “Delete” key.  

 

To install a new version, extract all files from the MH SAM PE.app zipped file.

1. Open the iPad Configuration Utility

2. Plug your iPad in

3. Click on the section on the left entitled “Applications”

4. Click the “Add” button at the top of the screen

5. Navigate to the unzipped MH SAM PE.app folder, select it, and then click “OK”

 

New Release Configuration Utility File Navigation

 

6. Click on your iPad (listed under “Devices” on the left)

7. Click the “Applications” tab at the top of the right hand pane

8. Click the “Install” button next to “MH SAM PE”

 

Installing a New Release of MH SAM PE on your iPad

 

9. The app should now appear on your iPad’s home screen
